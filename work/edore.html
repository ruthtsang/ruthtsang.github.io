Edore.org

Building a motivating community for learning and teaching

Edore is an ed-tech start-up that aspire to motivate people to learn by relating knowledge with real world problems. It is a place that helps teachers create, distribute and share their own exercise materials to students and other educators around the world.


Getting started

I had the amazing opportunity to work with the founding team to design the site from the beginning. As the first and only designer on the team, I was responsible in every aspects of the site from UI/UX to logo to illustrations to copywriting. I was asked to design the site based on the prototype they were building with Bootstrap. Since the structure of the site is pretty complex with a lot of functions, it's important to create a clean visual system that is consistent throughout the site.

The visual language

Since the structure of the site is pretty complex with a lot of functions, it's important to create a clean visual system that is consistent throughout the site. The homepage is visually heavy and image-driven. Users can discover relevant content quick and easy when they scroll.

Content Development

Besides the site design, I also created this long landing page with all our content on, about how it works. It’s a wonderful opportunity to learn how to narrow the content and think of what the audience need to know about a new digital product.
