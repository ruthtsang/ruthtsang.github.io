Hello!

My name is Ruth Tsang. I was born and raised in Hong Kong, spent my college time in Baltimore. After graduating from Maryland Institute College of Art, I spent the past two years designing websites and social media campaigns at Guru Online and Isobar. I also designed Edore.

What's in my mind?

I spent a lot of time thinking how design and people are related. As our world becomes more technically driven, the importance of people is more than it's ever been.

I enjoy working in collaboration with other designers/thinkers/artists because it stimulates the creative process. It is always excited to mix the people and ideas. I am happy to grab a coffee or beer with you to hear your crazy ideas.


I like a lot of things.

I enjoy reading on a beach, people watching at a cafe, exploring cities I've never been. I also enjoy working at night when everyone is asleep.

I like sunny days, burgers, maps, surfing, biking, crime dramas, having fun in life.
