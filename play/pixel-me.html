Pixel Me

My clothing journal in pixels

Pixel Me is my experimental art project to document my daily outfit in pixel form from June to August in 2012. Instead of keeping a written journal, my clothing journal helped me to record my life events.

This is an ongoing project. I decided to continue to document my daily outfit in 2014 but will present them in a different way - potentially an interactive infographic.
